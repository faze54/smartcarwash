Frequently Asked Questions

Does your water leave white spots?

No! We use high-quality well water which is naturally soft compared to municipal water. This ensures a spot-free finish and protects your paint from scaling.

How long does a full wash take?

A standard body wash takes about 30-40 minutes. A full interior and exterior detail takes about 60-90 minutes depending on how dirty the car is.

Do you clean under the chassis?

Yes, we perform thorough under-chassis washing to remove mud, sand, and salt accumulated from the roads.
